# Autonomous Vehicle Crash Data

We looked into whether any AV crash datasets could supplement our severity analysis with San Jose–specific records.

## What We Found

The CA DMV publishes AV incident reports, and a compiled version is available on figshare:
[Autonomous Vehicles Crash and Disengagement Report (CA DMV)](https://figshare.com/articles/dataset/Autonomous_Vehicles_Crash_and_Disengagement_Report_CA_DMV_/16923463)

It's the only source we found that includes a city-level label, which would let us filter for San Jose crashes.

## Why We Didn't Use It

The dataset covers 2014–2020 and has **259 total records** across San Francisco and San Jose combined. That's too small to draw any meaningful correlations or train a reliable model on.

## Conclusion

Skipped for now. If the DMV dataset grows substantially in future years, it could be worth revisiting for an AV-specific severity sub-analysis.
